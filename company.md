# HiveDemo

HiveDemo is a cutting-edge AI software company specialized in building "AI Operating Systems". 

## Mission
Our mission is to enable businesses to run themselves through autonomous agent networks.

## Core Products
1. **HIVE AI OS**: A multi-agent system that coordinates complex engineering and product management tasks.
2. **Trigger Engine**: An autonomous event-driven system that allows agents to act without human intervention based on schedules, thresholds, or events.
3. **Policy Engine**: A governance layer that defines safety and budget rules for autonomous agents.

## Operations
We operate a monorepo containing multiple services:
- **API**: The central goal-submission hub.
- **Agents**: Specialized workers (PM, Dev) that execute tasks.
- **Orchestrator**: Manages the flow and state of complex goals.
- **Frontend**: A Phaser-powered game-like visualization of agent activity.
